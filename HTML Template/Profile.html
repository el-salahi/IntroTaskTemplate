Let's work together!

Here are a few of the values I feel create an effective working enviroment

  • Establishing clear and consistent communcation is vital for any collaboration
  • No question is a stupid question. It's important to foster an environment in which everyone feels comfortable enough to be curious. It's the only way we learn!
  • Provide the context. Whether its an assignment, a request or a question.
  • I appreciate constructive feedback. A structure I find particularly useful is the WIN model:
    Working - what's working well?
    Improvements - what could you improve?
    Next - what will you do differently next time?
  • An open line of communication is key. Find a list of my prefed methods of getting in touch at the bottom of the page
