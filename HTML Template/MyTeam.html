Software Development Team Roles and Responsibilities

My Role

I am a junior software engineer within a full stack team. I contribute to both front-end and back-end components under the guidance of more experienced team members. Some of my responsibilities are as follows:

  • A significant portion of my responsibilities involves writing, maintaining, and testing code across our team's services and applications. I adhere to the coding standards and best practices established by my organization. I do this under guidance from senior team members, who help me understand the codebase and the architectural decisions that have been made.
  • Testing is a key part of my role. I contribute by writing unit tests to ensure adequate code coverage, performing basic manual testing, and supporting the development and maintenance of our integration test suite to help ensure reliability across systems.
  • I participate in code reviews, where I both provide feedback to peers and, more importantly as a junior team member, receive feedback on my own work. This process helps me continually improve the quality of the code I produce.

Senior Software Engineer (C5)

Senior Software Engineers are responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining complex systems across the full stack. They lead technical decision-making, and are often the point of contact for cross-functional team collaboration. They also support junior engineers by providing guidance and sharing best practices. These additional responsibilies often lead to less time in the code-base, but instead a focus on architecture and design.

Sundeep, Senior Engineer

Sundeep is a senior engineer in our team and his expertise lies in back-end technologies. He is instrumental in technical design and architectural discussion-making, ensuring that our solutions are robust and scalable. He also fosters continuous learning within the team through code reviews and mentorship for myself and other engineers.

Software Engineer (C4)

Software Engineers are responsible for building, testing, and maintaining our software applications across the stack. They contribute to system design, write clean and efficient code, and typically take ownership of specific features or components. They take the lead on code contributions as well as code reviews and help ensure that development follows best practices.

QA Tester

QA (Quality Assurance) Testers are responsible for ensuring that software meets the required quality standards before it's released. They design and execute test plans, perform both manual and automated testing, and identify bugs or issues early in the development process. QA testers work closely across a whole team - with Product and UX to understand requirements and define acceptance criteria, and with developers to then validate the user experience and functionality of the software.

Karthika, QA Engineer

Karthika is the QA engineer on our team, ensuring comprehensive test coverage for our application, giving our team confidence in every deployment. Karthika collaborates closely with developers like myself to identify and resolve issues as part of our agile process. All front-end development in our application must pass through her testing environment for validation and approval before release.

Product Owner

Product Owners are responsible for defining and prioritizing the features and requirements of our software products. They own the product backlog, work closely with stakeholders to gather input, and ensure that the development team delivers value that aligns with business goals. Product Owners play a key role in sprint planning, clarify requirements, and help guide the team toward delivering impactful, user-focused solutions.

UX Designer

UX Designers are responsible for creating intuitive, user-centered experiences across our applications. They conduct user research and usability testing to ensure designs are accessible, usable, and consistent. Based on business requirements, they produce wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs, and clearly communicate their design decisions to stakeholders as well as the development team.

Lisa, UX Designer

Lisa is the UX designer on our team, focused on delivering seamless, accessible, and engaging user experiences. She works closely with Product and stakeholders to understand and translate business requirements into thoughtful, user-focused designs. She also collaborates with developers like myself to ensure that design intent is clearly communicated and reflected in the final product.
